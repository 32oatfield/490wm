The above map features a locations of my favorite business locations in Washington DC. The map features an array of Theatres, Bars, Bicycle, and Music shops. The 'icon' of the point on the point specifcies what type of business it is.

For your convenience, the checkmark list on the side allows the ability to 'sort the map' by unchecking undesired business types.

P.S. - if it wasn't obvious, the points shown on this map have been fictionalized/made up. The point of creating this page was so that students (like me) in the GEOG490 Web Mapping Class at the University of Oregon could gain experiance working with scale and putting a mapbox map into a HTML template! This is one of our class assignments.
